Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail

Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail is the world’s most expensive car, priced at $30 million (Rs. 211 crore approx). Contrary to Rolls Royce’s earlier four-seater layout, La Rose Noire is a two-seater supercar with a removable hardtop.The Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail is equipped with a twin-turbo 6.75-liter V-12 engine, giving power of 563 bhp with a peak torque of 820 Nm. Its body is made out of carbon, steel, and aluminum.

Bugatti La Voiture Noire

Bugatti La Voiture Noire" is a one-of-a-kind hypercar created by the luxury automotive brand Bugatti. Launched at the Geneva International Motor Show in 2019, it is a modern interpretation of the iconic Bugatti Type 57 SC Atlantic. The name "La Voiture Noire" translates to "The Black Car" in English, paying homage to the original car. With a price tag of around $18.68 million, it is one of the most expensive new cars ever built. The hypercar boasts a powerful 8.0-liter quad-turbo W16 engine, producing a staggering 1,479 horsepower and is capable of reaching a top speed of over 260 mph.

Pagani Zonda HP Barchetta

The Pagani Zonda HP Barchetta is a limited-production supercar manufactured by the Italian sports car manufacturer Pagani. It features a powerful engine, impressive aerodynamics, and a unique design. Only three units of the Zonda HP Barchetta were made, making it a highly exclusive and sought-after vehicle among car enthusiasts and collectors.

Mercedes-Maybach Exelero

The Mercedes-Maybach Exelero is an ultra-exclusive, high-performance luxury sports car developed by Mercedes in 2005. It features a powerful 690 hp twin-turbo V12 engine, can reach over 218 mph, and accelerate 0-60 mph in just 4.4 seconds. With its striking, futuristic design and a price tag over $8 million, the single Exelero model produced is one of the most expensive and rare production cars ever built.

Bugatti Divo

The Bugatti Divo is a limited-production sports car manufactured by Bugatti Automobiles S.A.S. It was first introduced in 2018 and is known for its high performance and exclusivity. The Divo is based on the Bugatti Chiron but features a more aggressive design with improved aerodynamics. It is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine that produces around 1500 horsepower, allowing it to reach top speeds of over 200 mph. Bugatti only produced a limited number of Divos, making it a highly sought-after and rare car among automotive enthusiasts.
